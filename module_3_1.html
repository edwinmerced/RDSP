Practice


Module Practice Assessment 3.1.1 Many factors affect time management and are beneficial. Stephen Covey created a set of habits that focus on principles and values affecting a leader’s ability to be successful. This assessment will require that you identify Stephen Covey’s 7 habits of highly successful people.

Module Practice Assessment 3.1.1

This assessment will will consist of a multiple choice quiz. You will have to identify Stephen Covey’s 7 habits of highly successful people. You may complete this ungraded practice activity as many times as you wish.

General instructions to complete this activity

To complete this activity:

  1. Review the learning materials of this module.
  2. Press the "Go to Activity" button.
  3. Follow the instructions to complete the activity.

Assess


Module Summary: In this module you examined concepts used by hospitality management. You explored the concepts of organization, strategic planning, structure and execution.

Module Exit Assessment 3.1

This assessment will consist of a discussion forum. You will have to illustrate key components of strategic planning and time management. You will also provide examples of how you have applied them to your daily routine.

General instructions to access the assessment

To complete this assessment:

  1. Review the module learning materials and perform the practice exercise prior to taking this assessment.
  2. Create, review and save your illustration on the the key components of strategic planning and time management. This illustration will have to be added to your participation in the discusion forum on your application of the key components of strategic planning and time management.
  3. Press the "Go to Exit Assessment" button.
  4. Follow the instructions to complete your module exit assessment.
