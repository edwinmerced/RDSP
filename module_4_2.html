Learn


Module Introduction. Module introduction goes here
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Demonstrate knowledge of the housekeeping operations (safety standards, laundry management, environmental impacts and inventory controls)
  • Demonstrate knowledge of the front office operations (front office procedures, front office management, front office accounting and front office audit)
  • Demonstrate knowledge of the food and beverages operations (table service, beverage service and banquets event management)
  • Demonstrate knowledge of marketing (market segments and revenue management)
  • Demonstrate knowledge of the key minor operating departments (human resources and security)

Module Practice Assessment 4.2.1 Housekeeping extends beyond the cleanliness of guest rooms and public areas. The department is critical in its role to provide a safe work environment for guests and employees alike. This assessment will require that you demonstrate your knowledge of the key safety categories by identifying the hazard precaution practices exhibited by a housekeeping team.
