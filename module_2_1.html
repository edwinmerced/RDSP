Competency 2: Professionalism

Learning Module 2.1: Trends in Global Travel and Tourism


What is globalization? What impact does globalization have in Miami, Florida and the U.S.? What are the hospitality industry segments?
Photo Credit: iStockPhoto/gilaxia


Learn


Module Introduction. Globalization is the process by which businesses or other organizations develop international influence or start operating on an international scale. Globalization has an impact on Miami, Florida and the U.S. Globalization impacts each hospitality industry segment: food and beverages, travel and tourism, lodging and recreation.
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Understand the definition of globalization
  • Understand the impact of globalization in Miami
  • Identify the various hospitality industry segments
