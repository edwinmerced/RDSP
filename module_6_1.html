Learn


Module Introduction. Five specific catalysts of change have an impact on the travel and hospitality industry: the economy, platforms, consumer mindsets, public policy and enabling technology. Technology innovation in the travel and hospitality industry have includes innovative devices and applications. Mobile technology has also been a technological disruptor for the industry. Other technological innovations that will impact the industry include: artificial intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), and transportation ridesharing.
Learning Targets. Upon the completion of this module you will be able to:

  • Identify computer resources to manage the customer relationship experience
