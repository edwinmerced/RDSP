Program Introduction


Introduction to Program. This Rooms Division Specialist Certificate Program is designed to build on your knowledge of the rooms division segment within the hospitality industry. The program will cover content related to front office operations, housekeeping, laundry, guest services, leadership, and industry trends. This certificate program will provide you with industry recognized academic credentials.
Competencies. Upon the completion of this program you will be able to master competencies in the following areas:

  • Communications
  • Professionalism
  • Time Management
  • Knowledge Acquisition
  • Customer Service
  • Technology
